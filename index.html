Controls: Up and down arrow keys to move, spacebar to shoot.

Additional Controls:

  • Press 'd' for debug mode to see enemy hitboxes and lives.
  • Press 'p' to pause the game and 'r' to resume the game.

Power Up: Enter power-up mode by colliding with a small power-up fish (glowing) or by shooting fast Moonfish (2 light bulbs on stalks) from a distance.

Win Condition: Reach a score over 60 within 30 seconds to win the game!

Performance Note: If you experience any performance issues or lag on older computers, please let me know. This game is not optimized for mobile phones.
